Logo Inspiration.

The inspiration for this logo comes from a simple, minimalist idea. From researching other successful brands, it is clear that the less complicated the logo is, the better.

How the logo shows what the company is about.

This logo shows that the company is simple, yet detailed. This can be applied to their chocolate products as well.

Why the logo is effective for the brand.

This logo is effective because it provides a clear message, is simple, and can be regonized without having the words accompanied with it.

How the branding/logo makes us stand out.

This logo is unlike any other in the chocolatier market. This branding website also stands out to people and competitors because it has a unique, easy to read layout.
